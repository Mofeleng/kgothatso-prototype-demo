Kgothatso

Prototype Demo – AI-Powered Diagnostic Assistant

This interactive prototype showcases how users can engage with Kgothatso, an AI-powered assistant designed to improve access to healthcare in South Africa.

How to interact:

  • 1. Use the green Call button to simulate starting a USSD session.
  • 2. Tap anywhere on the loading screen to begin.
  • 3. Click the Send buttons to proceed through the chatbot flow.

For best results, use fullscreen mode and ensure your browser is updated.
